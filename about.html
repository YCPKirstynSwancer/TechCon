About

DevCon came out of the collective ideas of the Central Pennsylvania Technology Council and Harrisburg University as a way to build community in the region and attract top talent in the area.

Why Come?

  1. Open your mind to fresh thoughts and new ideas.

  2. Networking with peers, potential hires, or new opportunities.

  3. Identify new partners.

  4. Recognize that your not alone with your challenges and get help.

  5. See emerging technology in action.

  6. Why just attend? Speak, or join a panel!

  7. Pick an analyst's brainBrainstorm and innovate!

  8. Brainstorm and innovate!

  9. Share on social media and with your colleagues.

  10. Have some fun!
